How to Use — ASL Translator
1Open the ASL Translator tab and allow camera access.
2Position yourself so your hands are clearly visible in the camera frame. Good lighting helps.
3Wait for the status to show Ready, then perform one of the 6 supported signs.
4Hold the sign steady for about 1 second. The app will speak the recognized word aloud.
5The confidence bars at the bottom show what the AI is detecting in real time.
6Lower your hands fully between signs to reset detection.
Tip: Signs that use two hands (DOCTOR, HELP, PAIN) require both hands to be visible in frame.
How to Use — Communication Board
1Open the Communication Board tab.
2Choose a category from the top row: Yes/No, Needs, Pain, Comfort, or Body.
3Tap any phrase button — the app will speak it aloud immediately through your device speaker.
4No camera or sign language knowledge needed. Any patient can use this instantly.
Tip: Turn your device volume up before handing it to a patient.
